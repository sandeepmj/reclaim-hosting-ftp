FTPing onto ReclaimHosting Servers

SFTP password

Check your welcome email from ReclaimHosting for your secure FTP (SFTP) password

Log in to your account

NOTE: Do NOT use the credentials you received in your email for SFTP. This is the user name and password you created when you joined ReclaimHosting.

Log in with your credentials

This is your admin landing

Click on cPanel for your domain (takeoffpad.com)

This is your cPanel, but you will have to scroll down....

...to the FTP Accounts

Scroll down to Special FTP Accounts

Look for the username that you found in your welcome email and then click on Configure FTP Client

Select CyberDuck for Mac SFTP Configuration file

Locate the CyberDuck config file in your downloads folder and double-click it.

This launches CyberDuck with the proper configuration. Add your password.

You're connected.

Your folders will NOT be the same as the ones shown below.

Double-click on public_html.

Finding the public URL

I have added a project folder to the malik folder. Double click malik to find the folder.

The public url in this case is www.takeoffpad.com/malik/myfruitproject/
